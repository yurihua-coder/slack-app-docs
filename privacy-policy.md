# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 27, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle data when you use our application (the "App") with Slack.

## Data Collection

Our App accesses the following types of data from your Slack workspace:

- **Bot User Information**: Basic bot identity and permissions
- **Workspace Information**: Workspace ID and name for identification
- **Message Content**: Only messages in channels where the bot is explicitly added

## Data Usage

The collected data is used solely for:
- Providing core functionality of the App
- Responding to user commands and interactions
- Maintaining operational logs for debugging purposes

## Data Storage

- Data is stored securely and access is restricted
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for the App's functionality

## Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except:
- When required by law
- With your explicit consent
- To trusted service providers who assist in operating the App (under confidentiality agreements)

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security reviews

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access data we have collected about your workspace
- Request deletion of your data
- Revoke the App's access at any time via Slack's app management

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in this repository and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in our repository.
